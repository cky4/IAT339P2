Health & Performance

Nanaimo's Optimal Health & Performance Centres

Get back to feeling great and living life fully. Let the expert practitioners at our Nanaimo health and performance centres assess your injury or chronic pain and help you get back to your job, your sports, your life.

At Island Optimal, you can work with several practitioners who are experts in their various fields. Having all that expertise in one clinic allows us to better diagnose and more quickly treat your musculoskeletal injuries and conditions.

Every treatment plan is unique. In some cases, pairing chiropractic with massage or naturopathic with yoga can yield the best results for healing.

Boost your fitness and athletic performance

Whether you are looking for weight loss, improved sports or physical performance, or even just a healthier lifestyle, the fully certified training team at Island Optimal Performance can provide the guidance, support, motivation, and encouragement needed to not only achieve your goals but surpass them!

Experience Circuit, Strength & Conditioning, and Cross Training group sessions, popular fitness classes, private, personal and Team Training, as well as supervised open gym training with structured daily programs all in our new +5000sq' high-end fitness facility!
