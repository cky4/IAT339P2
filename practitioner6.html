Anika Miehe

Anika Miehe | PhysicoTherapist

Information

B.HK (Hons), D.C., C.S.C.S., ART®

Anika was born in Montreal, Quebec. Growing up in a German family of four siblings she learnt to speak German, French as well as English. She played many sports before finding a passion in Volleyball, playing as a middle hitter for a team in Northern Germany during a high school year and being captain of the volleyball team during two years during Cegep in Montreal.

Anika recently moved to Nanaimo to be part of the Island Optimal Health & Performance Team and looks forward to new adventures on the Island.

Anika completed a Bachelor of Science in Athletic Therapy at Concordia University, Montreal (2011). Thereafter, she pursued her studies in the graduate program of Physical Therapy at Mcgill University to complete her Masters (2014). Professional Affiliations

Having worked with multiple athletic teams such as the Female Hockey Team of McGill University she has a lot of experience in the prevention and management of sports injuries as well as appropriate return to play planning.
