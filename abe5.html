Dr. Clare Craig

Dr. Clare Craig | Naturopathic Doctor

Information

NATUROPATHIC PHYSICIAN | ND, BA (Hons), CD(DONA)

Dr. Clare Craig is a registered, licensed doctor of naturopathic medicine in British Columbia. She trained at the accredited Canadian College of Naturopathic Medicine in Toronto, Ontario, and was graduated in May of 2006. Her clinical year brought with it valuable experience working with low-income individuals and families, First Nations communities, and HIV/AIDS patients, in addition to her regular rotation at the Robert Schad Naturopathic Clinic, the largest in North America.Clare has a strong desire to help all patients connect to the healing properties of nature and of their own bodies to assist them in finding balance and achieving health and well-being. She has a special interest in women's health, pediatric concerns, and general family practice. Clare is an eclectic practitioner and accesses a wide range of treatment modalities in order to best suit the individual needs of her patients.

In the years leading up to her role as Naturopathic Doctor, Clare earned an Honours Bachelor of Arts degree in Sociology and Anthropology from the University of Guelph. She then worked with community organizations, women’s groups, youth programs, and abroad before beginning her Naturopathic training. She traveled and worked in Africa and Central America, practicing French and Spanish as best as she could. Her travels brought unique learning experiences which continue to inform her approach to living as balanced and fulfilled a life as possible.
