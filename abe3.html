Dr. Joe Foglia

Dr. Joe Foglia | Chiropractor

Information

B.HK (Hons), D.C., C.S.C.S., ART®

Dr. Foglia graduated from the Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College in 2010, on the Dean's List with Clinic Honors. He also holds a Bachelor of Human Kinetics degree, Honors Movement Science with a minor in Human Biology from the University of Windsor. Joe graduated from the U of Windsor in 2006 as the Valedictorian and a CIS Academic All-Canadian, while competing for the varsity Men's Track and Field Team.

As a relatively new British Columbia resident in Aug. 2010, Joe has quickly immersed himself in the incredible outdoor activities and athletic organizations the City of Nanaimo and Vancouver Island have to offer. Joining the Island Optimal Health & Performance Team has been a perfect fit for Dr. Joe. He adds his passion for healthy living & patient-centered care to a team of outstanding multi-disciplinary health care practitioners, with an already well-developed reputation in the Nanaimo community. Dr. Joe thrives on having a positive impact on people's lives of all ages and activity levels, which consistently includes treating some of the area's top elite athletes and more recently was the Team Doctor & Medical Director for Team Canada at the 2013 Deaflympic Summer Games in Sofia, Bulgaria.
